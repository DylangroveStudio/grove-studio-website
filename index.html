ABOUT

Grove Studio is Melbourne's leading boutique architecture practice specialising in high-end residential projects. Founded by Dylan Grove in 2010, our studio brings a personal approach to every project, combining mid-century modern influences with contemporary functionality.

PROFESSIONAL CREDENTIALS

Grove Studio brings together a wealth of experience across Melbourne's leading studios. Dylan Grove is registered with the Architects Registration Board of Victoria, and is a member of the Australian Institute of Architects (AIA).

DESIGN PHILOSOPHY

Enduring Quality

We believe architecture should transcend time, creating spaces that feel equally relevant and functional across decadesâ€”not just when initially completed.

Context Sensitivity

Every project responds thoughtfully to its specific environment, whether urban, coastal, or rural, respecting the character of place while enhancing its potential.

Personalised Solutions

We reject one-size-fits-all approaches, instead developing deeply customised designs that reflect each client's unique lifestyle, preferences, and aspirations.
