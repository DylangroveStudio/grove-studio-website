ABOUT

Dylan Grove Portrait

With experience across Melbourne's design landscape, Dylan brings a collaborative approach to every project. His understanding of Australian residential architecture is complemented by an appreciation for how thoughtful design transforms both spaces and lives.

Professional Credentials

Dylan holds a Master's in Architecture from Melbourne's top program, licensed with the Architects Registration Board of Victoria since 2016. His work has been recognized through multiple awards and publications, establishing his position as a sought-after voice in contemporary Australian design.

Design Philosophy

Authentic Modernism

I believe in honoring mid-century modern principles of clean lines, honest materials, and indoor-outdoor connection while adapting them to contemporary Australian contexts.
