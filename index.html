Shoreham House

The Brief

With this 1950s coastal home, our clients sought to enhance functionality while honouring the mid-century character they loved. Working within a defined budget, they needed strategic interventions that would transform key spaces without compromising the home's existing charm.

The Outcome

Through thoughtful, light-touch interventions, we created a home that works harder while maintaining its distinctive mid-century appeal. The master bedroom now features custom full-height storage with integrated technology, while the study offers versatile functionality for both work and guests. The laundry was completely reconfigured with dedicated zones for beach gear and pet supplies, complemented by a refreshed bathroom that maintains practical features while introducing modern elements. The transformation exemplifies how targeted design can dramatically enhance daily living without costly comprehensive renovations.

Our Approach

A Transparent Process Built Around Your Vision

Clear communication consistently ranks as clients' most valued quality in an architect. Our structured yet flexible process keeps you informed at every stage while ensuring your vision guides all design decisions. We've developed this approach specifically to make the journey from concept to completion as stress-free and enjoyable as possible.

Discovery Package ($2,495 + GST, +$600 for regional VIC)

Our relationship begins with in-depth conversations about your vision, lifestyle, and practical needs. We take time to understand how you use your spaces and what matters most to you.

During this phase, we'll:

  • Visit your site to analyze its unique characteristics, opportunities and constraints
  • Discuss your aesthetic preferences through visual references
  • Establish a realistic budget and timeline
  • Review relevant planning regulations and building requirements

The discovery phase concludes with a comprehensive brief that captures your vision and sets clear parameters for the design process.

Project Coordination & Concept Design

This is where your vision begins to take shape. We explore multiple design directions to find the optimal solution for your needs, site, and budget.

During concept design, we develop:

  • Schematic floor plans and spatial arrangements
  • Initial material and aesthetic direction
  • Preliminary 3D visualizations to help you understand the spaces
  • Early sustainability considerations and strategies

We'll present options for you to consider, discuss the pros and cons of each approach, and refine the preferred concept based on your feedback.

Design Development

Once the concept is approved, we refine all aspects of the design in greater detail, making sure everything works together harmoniously.

This phase includes:

  • Detailed floor plans, elevations, and sections
  • Material selections and finishes
  • More sophisticated 3D visualizations
  • Integration of structural, mechanical, and electrical systems
  • Preliminary cost estimates from potential builders

The development phase ensures that the design is both beautiful and buildable, with all systems and details resolved.

Town Planning (if required)

For projects requiring planning approval, we prepare and manage the submission process.

Our town planning services include:

  • Preparation of all required documentation
  • Liaison with council planners
  • Management of the application process
  • Responses to any requests for information
  • Representation at council meetings if required

We aim to streamline this process while maximizing your project's potential within planning constraints.

Documentation

With the design fully resolved, we prepare comprehensive documentation for building permits, contractor bidding, and construction.

Documentation includes:

  • Detailed construction drawings and specifications
  • Material and finishes schedules
  • Coordination with engineering consultants
  • Building permit application assistance
  • Tender documentation for contractor selection

Clear, thorough documentation is essential for accurate pricing and smooth construction. We take pride in our meticulous attention to detail in this phase.

Construction & Onsite Presence

Our involvement continues throughout construction to ensure the design is properly executed and to address any challenges that arise.

During delivery, we provide:

  • Regular site visits and construction oversight
  • Resolution of design questions from the builder
  • Review of shop drawings and material samples
  • Quality control inspections at key milestones
  • Contract administration and payment certification

We remain your advocate throughout construction, working collaboratively with the builder while protecting your interests and maintaining design integrity.

About

The Studio & Our Approach

Personal Narrative

As an architect with a passion for mid-century modern principles, I bring a unique perspective to residential design that questions traditional spatial assumptions and creates homes that truly reflect how people live today.

My approach challenges conventional thinking about domestic spaces, drawing on MCM's emphasis on indoor-outdoor connection and functional beauty while adapting these principles for contemporary Australian living.

This perspective allows me to see possibilities others might miss – from reimagining transitions between spaces to creating homes that embrace both privacy and connection. Every design decision is guided by how it enhances your daily experience rather than simply following conventional patterns.

My connection to Melbourne's architecture community spans over a decade, with a deep appreciation for the city's unique blend of historical and contemporary influences. This local knowledge informs my practice and helps create homes that are distinctly Melbourne while embracing universal design principles.

Professional Credentials

With formal training from RMIT University, I've been a registered architect with the Architects Registration Board of Victoria since 2020. My work has been recognised through multiple industry awards, reflecting a commitment to excellence in residential design.

Professional affiliations include the Australian Institute of Architects, which keeps me connected to the latest developments in sustainable design practices and building technologies.

Continuing education in specialised areas like passive design, heritage adaptation, and contemporary materials ensures my approach remains innovative while grounded in practical expertise.

Design Philosophy

My design approach centres on three core principles:

Authentic Modernism

I believe in honouring MCM principles of clean lines, honest materials, and indoor-outdoor connection while adapting them to contemporary Australian contexts.

Livable Sustainability

Environmental consciousness should enhance daily experience, not compromise it. I integrate sustainable solutions that feel natural and elevate comfort.

Personalised Spaces

Your home should reflect your identity and support your lifestyle. My designs begin with understanding how you live and what matters most to you.
