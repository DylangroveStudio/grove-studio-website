Stage 1: Discovery Package

Our Discovery Package provides a structured first step to explore your project's potential, ensuring meaningful alignment between your aspirations and our expertise. For a fixed fee, you'll receive a comprehensive assessment and strategic framework tailored to your site and needs.

During this phase, we'll:

  • Visit your site to deeply understand your vision and lifestyle requirements
  • Explore your property's unique potential through expert analysis
  • Review planning regulations and building requirements that may impact your project
  • Create preliminary hand-sketched concepts that visualise possibilities
  • Present a beautifully bound document outlining our findings and recommendations
  • Discuss budget considerations and next steps with no pressure for immediate decisions

Stage 2: Project Coordination & Concept Design

We transform initial concepts into reality by bringing together practical considerations with creative vision. This phase bridges the gap between ideas and implementation.

Key inclusions:

  • Getting practical cost insights from builders
  • Refining project scope based on budget feedback
  • Developing schematic floor plans and spatial arrangements
  • Creating initial material and aesthetic direction
  • Producing preliminary 3D visualisations
  • Exploring sustainability considerations and strategies
  • Presenting design options with pros and cons of each approach
  • Refining preferred concept based on your feedback

Stage 3: Design Development

Once the concept is approved, we refine all aspects of the design in greater detail, ensuring everything works together harmoniously.

This phase includes:

  • Detailed floor plans, elevations, and sections
  • Material selections and finishes
  • More sophisticated 3D visualisations
  • Integration of structural, mechanical, and electrical systems
  • Preliminary cost estimates from potential builders

The development phase ensures that the design is both beautiful and buildable, with all systems and details resolved.

Stage 4: Town Planning (if required)

We prepare the application in response to statutory authority requirements, including plans, diagrams, analyses, studies, reports and other information, and formally lodge it as advised.

This phase may include:

  • Preparation of town planning documentation
  • Consultation with relevant authorities
  • Management of the application process
  • Responding to requests for information
  • Guidance through the approval process

Stage 5: Construction Documentation

With the design fully resolved, we prepare comprehensive documentation for building permits, contractor bidding, and construction.

Documentation includes:

  • Detailed construction drawings and specifications
  • Material and finishes schedules
  • Coordination with engineering consultants
  • Building permit application assistance
  • Tender documentation for contractor selection

Clear, thorough documentation is essential for accurate pricing and smooth construction. We take pride in our meticulous attention to detail in this phase.

Stage 6: Site Presence During Construction

Unlike many architects who hand off after documentation, we maintain an active presence throughout the construction process, ensuring your vision is properly executed.

Our site services include:

  • Regular site visits to monitor construction quality and progress
  • Addressing contractor questions and providing design clarifications
  • Reviewing shop drawings and material submissions
  • Resolving any unexpected issues that arise during construction
  • Conducting detailed inspections at key milestones
  • Facilitating clear communication between all stakeholders

Our continued involvement during construction provides peace of mind and ensures that the finished project truly reflects the design intent and quality you expect.

About Grove Studio

Meet Dylan Grove

Grove Studio was established by Dylan Grove, a registered architect (ARBV #20631) with a distinguished background spanning Melbourne's leading architectural practices and international study at the Royal Danish Academy of Fine Arts in Copenhagen.

Dylan's approach to architecture combines meticulous technical precision with an innate understanding of spatial quality and materiality. This refined methodology creates homes that respond authentically to both their occupants and natural context, establishing a harmonious dialogue between built form and environment.

With expertise across residential typologies from heritage transformations to contemporary new builds, Dylan's work is characterised by its thoughtful integration of refined aesthetics with practical intelligence. The result is architecture that elevates daily living – spaces that not only function beautifully but reveal subtle layers of consideration that enhance the experience of inhabitation.

Design Philosophy

At Grove Studio, we believe that exceptional architecture enhances daily life through thoughtful spatial arrangements, honest material expression, and considered environmental response. Each project begins by understanding the unique context and client aspirations, resulting in designs that are both timeless and distinctly personal.

Our approach is guided by Mid-Century Modern principles – clarity of form, integration with landscape, and attention to human scale – while addressing contemporary requirements for sustainability, flexibility, and technological integration.

We view architecture as a collaborative endeavour between client and architect, where open dialogue and mutual respect produce spaces that transcend mere buildings to become authentic reflections of the people who inhabit them.
